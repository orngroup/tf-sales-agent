One agent. One booking. Total ownership.

The Trip Factory Sales Agent is the single named point of contact for both the end client and the trade agent throughout the entire booking lifecycle. While specialist tasks — flight ticketing and visa processing — are handled by dedicated internal teams or outsourced partners, the agent retains full ownership and accountability for ensuring these are initiated, progressing on time, and completed correctly. No handoffs. No gaps. No excuses.

1
Phase 1
Sales & Consultation
Enquiry → confirmed booking

Enquiry handling

  • Respond to B2C and B2B leads within SLA
  • Qualify dates, group size, budget & all requirements
  • Identify dietary, accessibility & visa needs upfront
  • Log every enquiry in CRM with full detail immediately
  • Follow up every live enquiry — never let it go cold

Tour recommendation

  • Match client profile to the most suitable itinerary
  • Present guided, semi-guided & independent options
  • Upsell premium hotels, upgrades & experiences
  • Share a branded day-by-day itinerary PDF
  • Position TripFactory expertise vs competitors clearly

Quoting & negotiation

  • Build a fully itemised cost sheet with correct margins
  • Apply agent commission structures accurately
  • Provide a competitive quote with a validity period
  • Counter-negotiate without eroding margin
  • Offer tiered options at different price points

Closing the sale

  • Confirm all verbal agreements in writing by email
  • Collect deposit per the agreed payment schedule
  • Issue booking confirmation to client & agent same day
  • Open booking file in system immediately on deposit
  • Set balance payment deadline reminders in advance
2
Phase 2
Component Booking
Hotels · Transfers · Tours

Hotels & accommodation

  • Cross-check availability against every night of the tour
  • Book the correct room type, meal plan & occupancy
  • Confirm floor, non-smoking, view & special preferences
  • Request early check-in / late checkout where required
  • Add special notes: anniversary, honeymoon, medical

Transfers & ground

  • Book all airport, inter-hotel & inter-city transfers
  • Confirm correct vehicle type for group size & luggage
  • Coordinate cruise port & rail station pick-ups
  • Collect and share driver contact details pre-departure
  • Provide meet-and-greet sign board instructions

Tours, guides & experiences

  • Book escorted day tours with fully licensed guides
  • Pre-purchase tickets — skip-the-line where available
  • Confirm guide language matches client preference
  • Organise cruise, rail or coach passes as required
  • Arrange travel insurance for the full itinerary

Booking file & records

  • Maintain a complete, up-to-date booking file at all times
  • File all supplier confirmations against the booking
  • Flag any supplier unable to confirm within 24 hours
  • Record all amendments: date, reason, authorisation
  • Ensure every component confirmed before issuing invoice
3
Phase 3
Flight Coordination
Booked by flights team · Agent owns the outcome
Agent initiates

Brief the flights team

  • Submit a complete flight request to the flights team promptly
  • Include passenger names exactly as per passport
  • Specify airline preference, routing, cabin class & budget
  • Flag meal preferences, FF numbers & seat requirements
  • State the deadline by which tickets must be issued
Agent follows up

Chase & verify

  • Chase the flights team daily until confirmation received
  • Verify confirmed details match the original brief exactly
  • Check names, dates, routing & class are all correct
  • Confirm ticket numbers are on file before releasing to client
  • Monitor for schedule changes or involuntary re-routes
Agent communicates

Client communication

  • Confirm flight details to client promptly on ticketing
  • Ensure client understands check-in times & terminal info
  • Add e-tickets to the travel document pack
  • Action any client amendments through the flights team
  • Escalate pricing disputes or delays to management
i
Note: Flights are ticketed by the internal flights team, not the sales agent. However, the agent is fully responsible for initiating the request, chasing completion, verifying accuracy, and ensuring e-tickets reach the client before departure. If tickets are wrong or delayed, accountability sits with the agent.
4
Phase 4
Visa Application Management
Outsourced processing · Agent owns the timeline
Agent initiates

Initiate the application

  • Identify all visa requirements at point of booking
  • Advise client on visa categories needed for the itinerary
  • Issue a visa invitation or sponsorship letter if required
  • Refer client to the approved external visa partner
  • Set the application start deadline on the booking file
Agent collects

Document collection

  • Provide client with the full document checklist
  • Chase client for outstanding documents within 48 hours
  • Review submitted documents for obvious errors
  • Confirm visa partner has received the complete set
  • Log the submission date & reference on the booking file
Agent tracks

Progress tracking

  • Follow up with the visa company at least twice per week
  • Record each update against the booking in CRM
  • Communicate progress or delays to the client proactively
  • Escalate any concerns to management at the 3-week mark
  • Never assume progress without written confirmation
Agent verifies

Completion & verification

  • Confirm visa has been granted and received by the client
  • Verify validity dates cover the full travel period
  • Check number of entries matches the itinerary
  • Add visa confirmation to pre-departure briefing checklist
  • Escalate any refusal immediately and explore alternatives
!
Important: Visa processing is handled by an external partner. The agent is accountable for initiating the process on time, tracking it proactively, and verifying the visa is granted and correct before the pre-departure briefing. A client travelling without a valid visa is a critical failure — this is never someone else's responsibility.
5
Phase 5
Confirmation & Documentation
Every detail verified before departure

Supplier confirmations

  • Chase every supplier for written confirmation of all services
  • Verify names, dates & room types vs original booking
  • Resolve every discrepancy before issuing client documents
  • Re-confirm all services 72 hours before departure
  • Escalate unresolved supplier issues to management

Travel document pack

  • Compile all vouchers, e-tickets & hotel confirmations
  • Produce a clear day-by-day itinerary in the client's language
  • Include hotel addresses in local script for taxis
  • Add emergency contacts, local SIM & currency guidance
  • Dispatch the complete pack at least 10 days before travel

Pre-departure briefing

  • Call client to walk through the full itinerary in detail
  • Confirm passport validity meets destination requirements
  • Confirm visa has been received — check dates & entries
  • Brief on local customs, weather & dress codes
  • Share direct WhatsApp as 24/7 in-trip emergency contact

Agent / trade handover

  • Send a complete booking summary to the trade agent
  • Confirm commission due & exact payment timeline
  • Share emergency escalation contact with the agent
  • Notify agent of any last-minute changes immediately
  • Ensure agent can confidently answer their client's questions
6
Phase 6
In-trip & Post-trip Support
Always reachable · Issues owned end to end

Issue resolution

  • Be reachable on WhatsApp throughout the travel window
  • Handle hotel complaints & room issues immediately
  • Re-arrange missed or failed transfers without delay
  • Re-book disrupted or cancelled flights promptly
  • Escalate critical issues to ops team & management

Customer queries

  • Answer questions on tours, timings & all logistics
  • Assist with on-ground restaurant & activity requests
  • Coordinate with local guides for amendments
  • Provide local emergency & medical facility guidance
  • Keep the client calm, informed & fully supported

Agent queries

  • Respond to trade agent queries within 2 working hours
  • Provide status updates on the client's trip on request
  • Relay on-trip incidents or changes to agent promptly
  • Handle agent-relayed complaints diplomatically
  • Manage agent expectations professionally throughout

Post-trip follow-up

  • Welcome client home with a warm personalised message
  • Actively request a Google or TripAdvisor review
  • Gather constructive feedback for the operations team
  • Log supplier performance issues for future reference
  • Initiate the re-sell conversation for next year's holiday
7
Phase 7
Financial Accountability
Agent is financially responsible at every stage

Cost build & margin control

  • Build a fully itemised cost sheet before every quote
  • Maintain the minimum required margin on every component
  • Never issue a quote without completing the full cost build
  • Review margin impact whenever any amendment is made
  • Escalate to management before accepting below-min margin

Client invoicing & collections

  • Issue the correct invoice immediately on booking confirmation
  • Track deposit and balance payment deadlines without exception
  • Send payment reminders 7 days and 2 days before due date
  • Follow up overdue payments assertively within 24 hours
  • Issue formal receipts immediately on every payment received

Supplier invoice validation

  • Validate every supplier invoice against the agreed rate
  • Flag any discrepancy to the supplier in writing before paying
  • Never approve an invoice with incorrect amounts
  • Ensure all supplier payments are processed on or before due date
  • Cross-check payment confirmation number against booking record

Zero-error & audit policy

  • Agent carries full personal accountability for all financial errors
  • Double-check every name, date, cost & reference before confirming
  • Complete the mandatory financial audit checklist 7 days pre-departure
  • Document all amendments: date, reason, authorisation & financial impact
  • Financial losses caused by agent error are reviewed at appraisal
