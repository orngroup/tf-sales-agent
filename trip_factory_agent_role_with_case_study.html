Trip Factory Sales Agent role framework with a live European tour example from the India office

Trip Factory Sales Agent

Single point of contact · Full-circle ownership · India office

Phase 1 Sales & Consultation

Enquiry handling

  • Respond to B2C and B2B leads within SLA
  • Qualify travel dates, group size, budget
  • Identify special needs — dietary, accessibility, visa
  • Log all enquiries in CRM with full notes

Tour recommendation

  • Match client profile to best-fit itinerary
  • Present guided vs independent options
  • Upsell premium hotels, business class, extras
  • Share itinerary PDF / digital proposal

Quoting & negotiation

  • Build accurate cost sheet with correct margins
  • Apply agent commission structures correctly
  • Provide competitive quote with validity period
  • Counter-negotiate without eroding margin

Closing the sale

  • Confirm verbal agreement via written summary
  • Collect deposit as per payment schedule
  • Send booking confirmation to client and agent
  • Open booking file in system immediately
Phase 2 Component Booking

Flights

  • Source best fares via GDS / NDC / consolidators
  • Book correct name, passport, meal preferences
  • Add frequent flyer numbers, seat selections
  • Monitor for schedule changes and re-routes

Hotels & accommodation

  • Cross-check availability against tour dates
  • Book correct room type, meal plan, occupancy
  • Confirm non-smoking, floor preferences
  • Request early check-in / late checkout if needed

Transfers & ground

  • Book all airport and inter-city transfers
  • Confirm vehicle type for group size
  • Coordinate port pick-ups for cruise legs
  • Share driver contacts with client before travel

Tours, guides & extras

  • Book escorted day tours with licensed guides
  • Arrange entrance tickets — skip-the-line where possible
  • Manage visa invitations, travel insurance
  • Coordinate cruise / rail passes as required
Phase 3 Confirmation & Documentation

Supplier confirmations

  • Chase every supplier for written confirmation
  • Verify names, dates, room types against booking
  • Resolve discrepancies before issuing documents
  • Re-confirm all services 72 hrs before departure

Travel document pack

  • Compile vouchers, e-tickets, hotel confirmations
  • Produce day-by-day itinerary in client language
  • Include emergency contacts, local SIM guidance
  • Send pack at least 10 days before departure

Pre-departure briefing

  • Call client to walk through entire itinerary
  • Confirm passport validity, visa requirements
  • Brief on local customs, weather, dress codes
  • Provide 24/7 emergency contact number

Agent / trade handover

  • Send booking summary to trade agent partner
  • Confirm commission due and payment timeline
  • Provide agent with emergency contact escalation
  • Notify agent of any last-minute changes
Phase 4 In-trip & On-trip Support

Issue resolution

  • Be reachable during client travel window
  • Handle hotel complaints, missed transfers
  • Re-book flights on cancellation immediately
  • Escalate to supplier management if needed

Customer queries

  • Answer client questions on tours, timings, logistics
  • Assist with on-ground restaurant / activity requests
  • Coordinate with local guides for amendments
  • Keep client calm and informed at all times

Agent queries

  • Respond to trade agent queries within 2 hrs
  • Provide status updates on their client's tour
  • Relay any on-trip changes back to agent promptly
  • Manage agent expectations professionally

Post-trip follow-up

  • Welcome client home with thank-you message
  • Collect Google / TripAdvisor review
  • Gather improvement feedback for ops team
  • Initiate re-sell conversation for next trip
Phase 5 Financial Accountability

Cost control & margin

  • Build cost sheets with all components itemised
  • Maintain minimum margin per booking type
  • Validate supplier invoices against agreed rates
  • Flag any rate discrepancies before payment

Invoicing & collections

  • Issue correct invoice to client / trade agent
  • Track deposit and balance payment deadlines
  • Follow up on overdue payments within 24 hrs
  • Issue receipts immediately on payment receipt

Supplier payments

  • Ensure supplier payments are made on time
  • Avoid cancellation due to non-payment
  • Cross-check payment confirmation with booking
  • Escalate urgent payments to accounts team

Zero-error policy

  • Agent bears accountability for any booking error loss
  • Double-check all names, dates, costs before confirm
  • Perform final audit 7 days before departure
  • Document all amendments with reason on file
Phase 6 Reporting & Continuous Improvement

Performance tracking

  • Log conversion rate on all enquiries handled
  • Report monthly booking value and margin
  • Track repeat bookings and referrals generated
  • Review error rate — target zero financial losses

Knowledge & product

  • Attend supplier webinars and product training
  • Keep European tour product knowledge current
  • Share market intelligence with team
  • Suggest new tours / routes to product team

Live example — European escorted tour

Mumbai-based agent books a 14-night Best of Europe tour for 2 pax

Client
Mr & Mrs Kapoor, Mumbai
Trade agent
Horizon Travels, Andheri
Tour
Best of Europe — 14 nights
Route
Paris · Swiss Alps · Rome
Depart
Mumbai (BOM) — 18 Jun
Total value
INR 4,80,000 (2 pax)
1 Enquiry received — day 1
Sell Agent Horizon Travels emails enquiry for a Kapoor couple: 14 nights Europe, June, budget INR 4.5–5L, vegetarian meals only, anniversary trip.
Agent opens CRM, logs full requirement. Calls agent back within 1 hour to clarify: no train legs preferred, 4-star hotels minimum, private transfers only.
2 Proposal sent — day 2
Sell Agent prepares a branded PDF itinerary: Paris 4N / Swiss Alps 4N (Interlaken) / Rome 4N + 2 travel days. Pricing includes flights (Air India BOM–CDG, FCO–BOM), 4-star hotels, private transfers, escorted day tours with English-Hindi guide. Margin locked at 18%. Quote sent same day with 7-day validity.
3 Booking confirmed — day 5
Sell Horizon Travels confirms, Kapoors pay 30% deposit (INR 1,44,000). Agent issues deposit receipt, opens booking file, sends written confirmation email to both client and agent. CRM updated: status → Confirmed.
4 Flights booked — day 6
Book Air India AI 131 BOM–CDG 18 Jun, AI 112 FCO–BOM 2 Jul. Names exactly as per passport. Vegetarian meal (VGML) added. Seat selections: 2 aisle seats together. Frequent flyer numbers added. Booking confirmed in Amadeus. Cost: INR 1,10,000 both pax.
5 Hotels booked — day 7
Book Paris: Hotel Duquesne Eiffel 4* — Superior Double, BB, 18–22 Jun. Interlaken: Hotel Metropole 4* — Deluxe Double, BB, 22–26 Jun. Rome: Hotel Quirinale 4* — Classic Double, BB, 26–30 Jun. All confirmations received. Anniversary note added to each reservation.
6 Transfers & tours booked — day 8
Book Private airport transfers booked at all 3 cities. Day tours confirmed: Versailles full-day, Jungfraujoch excursion, Rome Vatican & Colosseum skip-the-line. English-Hindi guide confirmed for Versailles and Rome days. Entrance tickets pre-purchased. Schengen visa invitation letter issued to Kapoors.
7 Balance payment & documents — day 30
Confirm Balance INR 3,36,000 collected from Horizon Travels. All supplier confirmations received and cross-checked. Day-by-day itinerary PDF compiled in English with hotel addresses in local script. Voucher pack, e-tickets, transfer vouchers assembled. Full pack sent to Kapoors and Horizon on 8 Jun — 10 days before departure.
8 Pre-departure call — day 40 (14 Jun)
Confirm 30-minute call with Mr & Mrs Kapoor. Walks through each day. Confirms Schengen visa received. Reminds: plug adaptors for Europe, currency mix (EUR cash + card), vegetarian restaurant list shared for each city. Agent's WhatsApp number provided as 24/7 in-trip contact.
9 In-trip issue — day 44 (18 Jun, Paris)
Support Mrs Kapoor WhatsApps at 10 PM IST: "Hotel says no reservation found." Agent checks confirmation — correct hotel, correct date. Calls hotel directly (Paris is 6:30 PM). Hotel locates reservation under slightly different name spelling. Issue resolved in 20 minutes. Agent updates CRM with incident log.
10 Agent query mid-trip — day 48
Support Horizon Travels asks for status update — their client is enjoying the trip but requested an extra night in Rome. Agent checks availability at Hotel Quirinale — available at INR 9,500 supplement. Quotes Horizon, gets approval, books immediately and issues updated voucher. Supplements collected within 24 hrs.
11 Post-trip follow-up — day 60
Support Kapoors return. Agent sends personalised WhatsApp: "Hope the anniversary was magical!" Requests Google review and collects brief feedback. Kapoors mention Jungfraujoch was the highlight; minor note on Rome transfer being slightly late. Feedback logged for supplier review. Agent re-sell: "Shall we plan Scandinavia or Amalfi for next year?"
12 Financial close — day 62
Finance Agent reconciles full booking file: all supplier invoices validated against agreed rates. Total cost: INR 3,93,600. Revenue: INR 4,89,500 (incl. Rome extra night). Net margin: INR 95,900 (19.6%). Horizon Travels commission processed. Final accounts submitted to finance. Booking closed — zero errors, zero financial loss.
